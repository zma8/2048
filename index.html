2048 game

How to Play

Use your arrow keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) to move the tiles.

When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one.

You can use Undo to go back one step - but you only have 3 undos per game.

Your goal is to create the 2048 tile — but you can keep playing to beat your score!
